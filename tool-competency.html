Competency Mapping

Build your complete competency landscape. List all your skills — professional and personal — rate your expertise level, and see where your real strengths lie.

How to use this tool

Set aside 15–30 minutes. List all your competencies — professional and personal. Be comprehensive rather than modest.

Prompts to help you think:

  • What's on your CV or LinkedIn?
  • What do you enjoy doing in your free time?
  • What do people come to you for advice on?
  • What comes naturally to you that others find difficult?
  • What hobbies have you had over the years?
  • What roles do you play — parent, team lead, mentor, organiser?
