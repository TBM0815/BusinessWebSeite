Cultural Fit Assessment

Living abroad means navigating two cultures: yours and theirs. This tool helps you understand where they align — and where they clash.

This assessment explores 10 cultural dimensions that shape how we work, communicate, and build relationships. You'll rate yourself first, then rate your host country based on your experience.

At the end, you'll see your cultural profile, discover your "cultural archetype," and understand where friction might arise.

Based on cross-cultural research frameworks (Erin Meyer, Hofstede, GLOBE Study)
