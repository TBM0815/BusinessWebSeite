About this assessment

This assessment uses the IPIP Big Five personality inventory, a scientifically validated public-domain measure of the five major personality dimensions.

The Big Five model is the most widely used framework in personality psychology and career development. Your results will show your standing on:

Extraversion – How you gain energy and interact with others

Agreeableness – Your approach to cooperation and harmony

Conscientiousness – Your organization and discipline

Emotional Stability – How you handle stress and uncertainty

Openness – Your creativity and curiosity

This takes about 10 minutes. Answer based on how you generally are now, not how you wish to be. Describe yourself honestly, in relation to other people of the same age and background as you.
