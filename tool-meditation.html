Meditation Timer

Sometimes the most productive thing you can do is stop. A simple timer to give your mind the space it needs to reset.

Instructions

1
Sit comfortably — back straight, shoulders relaxed
2
Close your eyes and breathe deeply through your nose
3
Focus on your breath — in and out, naturally
4
When thoughts arise — let them pass like clouds, gently return to your breath
A gong will gently bring you back at the end.
