Energy Audit

What gives you energy? What drains it? Track your activities, rate their energy impact from -5 to +5, and see how your weekly time translates into energy gains or losses. Honest input creates the most useful insights.

How it works

1. Select the activities that apply to you from the categories below

2. Rate each activity's energy impact from -5 (drains massively) to +5 (energizes massively)

3. Enter how many hours per week you spend on this activity

4. Add your own activities if something important is missing

The time input matters: an activity rated -5 that takes 20h/week drains you far more than one rated -5 that only takes 1h. Your total weekly energy impact is calculated as: energy score × hours per week.
