The Book

JUMP

Notizen eines Hamsters · by Tobias Münch

Most professionals don't leave the hamster wheel because they don't know how. JUMP is a practical guide that maps the journey from stuck to moving — built from real experience, not theory.

The four-phase JUMP method gives you a structure that is honest about how change actually works. Not linear, not painless — but navigable.

J

Where are you now, really?
U

M

P

About

The person
behind the work

I have spent over 20 years in the audio and consumer electronics industry — as an engineer, as a leader, as a team builder. Roles at Harman, Jabra and others have given me a foundation that is broader than most CVs suggest.

"I am at my best when I can build new or improve — not when I am managing what already exists."

In parallel, I have practised and taught Shinson Hapkido for 30 years. Martial arts sounds far from boardrooms and coaching conversations — but the principles transfer surprisingly well: clarity on goals, respect for the process, energy applied with precision.

From this combination came JUMP — and TeamworX. Because I have seen again and again how much potential sits dormant in people and teams, waiting to be activated.
